Referrals must include

  • Date of referral
  • Referring GP's name, address, provider number and HealthLink ID
  • Patient's full name, address, date of birth and contact number
  • Reason for referral

If available, please include

  • Patient's Medicare number
  • Private health fund details
  • Patient's email address
  • Relevant imaging, pathology and prior correspondence
All referrals are reviewed and triaged by Dr Robertson before an appointment is booked. Once triaged, our staff will notify the patient when to contact the clinic to schedule.
