Becoming a Patient

A clear path to your appointment

Step one

GP referral

Ask your GP to send a referral — electronically via HealthLink (kkarthig) or by fax. A referral is required for all appointments.

Step two

Review & triage

Every referral is reviewed by Dr Robertson and prioritised on clinical need. Our team will then contact you.

Step three

Your consultation

An unhurried first appointment at our Nedlands rooms — your history heard in full, your options explained clearly.

Please note: Dr Robertson is a private specialist. Private health insurance is required.
